Neurology Department

About Our Neurology Department

Our Neurology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our expert neurologists provide comprehensive care for a wide range of neurological conditions.

We utilize advanced diagnostic technologies and evidence-based treatments to manage conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, migraines, and neuromuscular disorders.

Our Services Include:

  • EEG (Electroencephalography)
  • EMG/NCS (Electromyography/Nerve Conduction Studies)
  • Brain and Spinal MRI
  • Stroke Care and Rehabilitation
  • Epilepsy Monitoring and Treatment
  • Headache and Migraine Management
  • Movement Disorder Treatment
