Cardiology Department

About Our Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for all heart-related conditions. We offer advanced diagnostic procedures, interventional treatments, and preventive care to ensure optimal heart health for our patients.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of highly skilled cardiologists, we are committed to delivering exceptional care for conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and valvular heart disease.

Our Services Include:

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Echocardiography
  • Stress Testing
  • Cardiac Catheterization
  • Angioplasty and Stent Placement
  • Pacemaker Implantation
  • Heart Failure Management
