Ophthalmology Department

About Our Ophthalmology Department

Our Ophthalmology Department provides comprehensive eye care services, from routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures. Our ophthalmologists are experts in diagnosing and treating all eye conditions and diseases.

We offer state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and surgical technology to ensure the highest quality care for our patients' vision needs, including cataract surgery, LASIK, glaucoma treatment, and retinal care.

Our Services Include:

  • Comprehensive Eye Exams
  • Cataract Surgery
  • LASIK and Refractive Surgery
  • Glaucoma Diagnosis and Treatment
  • Retinal Disease Management
  • Pediatric Ophthalmology
  • Emergency Eye Care
